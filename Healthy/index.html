The Three Golden Steps

These are the three golden steps that will lead you to success.

First 3 Days

During this step you begin your health journey by eating from a list of alkaline foods and starting your nutritional supplements. There is no need to calorie count for these 3 days. The focus of step 1 is to remove sugar and carbohydrates from your diet to begin to heal your gut. Step 1 prepares your body for Step 2.

Fat Loss Detox

This is the detox and weight loss step, and your meals will be calorie restricted. It is when your body will start to change as a result of burning fat. There is an easy to follow meal plan, and for anyone with dietary requirements a list of approved foods is also provided. The nutritional supplements are imperative during this step as they sustain your energy levels and curb your appetite (in addition to their other roles).

Maintenance

This is where we teach the body to keep the weight off. This is the final link in the chain where the gut is fully healed. There are some additional nutritional products, we re-introduce additional food groups into your diet, and increase the calories to begin to teach you how to live healthily for the rest of your life.

A Typical Day

This is how your daily plan may look like.

  • Daily

    Throughout the day you must consume 3 litres of water.

  • Morning

    First thing in the morning you take your nutrition, with some water and protein. Also, instead of morning tea you can have a fruit.

  • Afternoon

    At lunch time you have a few supplements, protein (chicken, fish or red meat) and ONE vegetable. Afternoon snack can be any fruit.

  • Dinner

    For dinner you have protein (not red meat this time) and ONE vegetable, plus some more supplements.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I have to buy this every month?

    No, our program sees you lose the weight and without realising you start a lifestyle change. Once the weight is off you dont need to keep taking our supplements. Depending on how many rounds you need to do is the only time you buy supplements.

  • I am a vegetarian, can I do the program?

    Yes – we have a seperate menu plan and supplements suitable for a vegetarian.

  • Can I eat red meat on the program?

    Yes you can eat red meat on the program. If you have an issue eating red meat you can then switch it out for another white meat off the list of approved foods.

  • Do I have to drink all the 3 liters of water?

    Yes, the water helps in suppressing your appetite and cleansing your gut.

  • Do you have any food recipes for the program?

    Yes, we have a separate facebook page filled with thousands of recipe ideas. We encourage our clients to add their own recipes as well.

  • I work FIFO can I do this program?

    Definitely, we have plenty of FIFO workers on the program and having great success.
